## Structural findings surfaced during Stage 1 (Setup + Phase 0)

**F3 (NEW, structural) — the Phase 0→1 gate is retrospective, and the local 0→1 advance is un-gated.**
`validate_manifesto_content` (check-phase-gate.sh L858) and the phase-0-intermediates
check (L864) are both inside `if [ "$current_phase" -ge 1 ]`. So a **bare**
`check-phase-gate.sh` at `current_phase=0` validates ONLY the Pre-Phase 0
pre-conditions — it never checks the manifesto, phase-0 files, or the 0→1 approval.
The prospective check requires `check-phase-gate.sh --gate phase_0_to_1` (BL-060
override → current_phase=1, non-mutating). AND `start_phase1()`
(process-checklist.sh L209) advances to phase 1 **without consulting any gate**
(`grep -c check-phase-gate` in the function → 0), unlike `start_phase3()` which
consults the bug gate. Net: an operator can `--start-phase1` with no manifesto at
all; the missing evidence is only caught retrospectively (bare gate once at phase 1)
or in CI at push. This extends X-010's "interlock at push, detector locally"
asymmetry to the 0→1 boundary. The walk used `--gate phase_0_to_1` for all
prospective 0→1 assertions (the checklist's D-16 assumed a bare invocation checks
the manifesto — it does not).
